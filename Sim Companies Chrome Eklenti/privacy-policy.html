Privacy Policy

Sim Companies Dashboard  ·  Last updated: April 3, 2026

🔒 This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

1. Overview

Sim Companies Dashboard ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension designed to help Sim Companies players visualize and analyze their in-game financial data. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is used.

2. Data We Access

The extension accesses the following data solely within your browser:

  • Sim Companies game data — Financial reports, balance sheets, cash flow records, warehouse inventory, and product data retrieved from the official Sim Companies API (simcompanies.com), which you are already logged into.
  • Browser Storage (chrome.storage) — Used exclusively to save your personal preferences such as theme (dark/light), language selection, and growth goal targets. This data never leaves your device.
  • Active Tab — The extension reads the active Sim Companies tab to retrieve your session data. No browsing history or other tab content is ever accessed.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • No payment or financial card information
  • No browsing history or data from other websites
  • No data is ever sent to third-party servers or our servers
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind

4. Third-Party Services

The extension loads the following external resources for functionality and display:

  • jsdelivr.net — Used to load a public Earth texture image for the animated globe on the loading screen. No user data is sent.
  • simcompanies.com API — The official game API is used to fetch your own in-game data. This is equivalent to browsing the game website normally.

5. Permissions Justification

  • storage — To locally save your UI preferences (theme, language, goals).
  • tabs — To detect and communicate with your open Sim Companies tab.
  • host_permissions (simcompanies.com) — To read your in-game financial data from the official game API while you are logged in.

6. Data Retention

All data processed by the extension exists only in your browser's local memory and storage. No data is persisted on any external server. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored preferences.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
