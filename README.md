Sim Companies Dashboard — Advanced Financial Analytics Panel
A powerful Chrome extension for Sim Companies players that aggregates all your company's financial data  
into one beautiful, interactive dashboard with a single click.
📊 FEATURES:
• OVERVIEW — Total assets, cash, stock, rank, and daily net profit at a glance
• GROWTH — Daily asset changes, cumulative growth, and trend charts
• BALANCE SHEET — 30-day balance table: cash, stock, buildings, patents, and totals
• CASH FLOW — Real cash flow statement: inflows, outflows, and net cash flow
• ASSETS — Current / non-current asset breakdown and 60-day stock trend
• PRODUCTS — Per-product revenue, COGS, gross profit, and margin analysis
• VWAP ANALYSIS — Profitability analysis of warehouse stock vs. market VWAP prices
• WAREHOUSE — Unit cost, market value, and valuation gap per product
• COMPANY CHART — Company value trend over 90/180 days or all time
• GROWTH GOALS — Set personal growth targets and track your progress
• DATA TABLE — Master data view: balance sheet, daily change, and asset structure
🌙 Dark / Light theme support
🌐 Turkish / English language support
Usage: Keep the simcompanies.com tab open and logged in, then click "LOAD DATA" in the extension.
